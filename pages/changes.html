May 3, 2025

You can now register and log into an account directly on the site. Once logged in, you’ll be able to leave a star rating and optional comment on any poem. These ratings contribute to an overall average shown beneath each poem title. We've also implemented tools to sort, search, and filter poems—helping you quickly find specific works by title, theme, or tag. These updates make the reading experience smoother and more personalized for every visitor.
